I am an affiliated postdoc fellow at Bremen International School of Social Sciences. Before this, I was a postdoc fellow at the Psychology & Methods department of Jacobs University in a project funded by Business Finland and Hofstede Insights. At the University of Oldenburg, I worked as the interim academic coordinator of European Master in Migration & Intercultural Relations, also as a lecturer at the Institute of Social Sciences.

During my undergrad studies, I worked as a Before academic life, I was a sales manager at a multi-national outdoor advertising company in Istanbul.

I am proficient in the broad applications of (1) the general structural equation modeling framework (2) psychometric networks (3) social network analysis (4) quantitative text analysis (5) machine learning. I actively work with R. I am Python literate. I turn to Mplus when necessary – that is rare, though.
